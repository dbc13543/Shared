Starting Remote Camera Control
Follow the procedures below to start Remote Camera Control:
1
Connect the camera and your computer with a USB cable.
For the detailed connection, please refer to the camera´s manual. Please use the camera with fully-charged batteries or AC adaptor.
2
Turn on your camera.
3 For Windows
Click [Start] at the bottom left of the Windows desktop, point to [All Programs], and then click [Remote Camera Control]-[Remote Camera Control].
Remote Camera Control starts.

For Mac
Start Finder, click [Applications], and then double-click [Remote Camera Control] in the [Remote Camera Control] folder.
Remote Camera Control starts.
If the [Select a Device] message appears, select the model name of your camera.
Quitting Remote Camera Control
Do either of the following to quit Remote Camera Control:
  • For Windows: Click the close button [X] at the upper right-hand corner of the[Remote Camera Control] window.
  • For Mac: Click [Quit Remote Camera Control] from the [RCC] menu.
Uninstalling Remote Camera Control
Do either of the following to uninstall Remote Camera Control from your PC hard disk:
  • For Windows: Click [Start] at the bottom left of the Windows desktop, and then click [Control Panel]-[Add/Remove Programs]-[Remote Camera Control].
  • For Mac: Start Finder, click [Applications], and then drag [Remote Camera Control] from the [Remote Camera Control] folder and drop it into the trash bin.
