Welcome to Remote Camera Control
What is Remote Camera Control?
"Remote Camera Control" is application software that allows you to control Sony digital cameras that support remote capture and are connected to your computer via USB. The thus shot still images are transferred to and saved in the specified folder of your computer automatically. You can also use Remote Camera Control to change shooting conditions such as shutter speed, exposure, or focus. Movies can only be shot (the shooting conditions cannot be changed).

For more information, refer to Remote Camera Control Help Guide (Additional Help Online).
