Shooting a movie
The operation panel of the Remote Camera Control window allows you to control a camera connected via USB to your PC and shoot a movie remotely. The captured movies are saved to the memory card of the camera.

1
Check to see that the camera settings are loaded in the operation panel.

If the camera settings are not displayed
Check to see if the camera is properly connected.
2
Click (MOVIE Button).

A movie is shot. The captured movies are saved to the memory card of the camera.
Notes
  • Configure settings for movie shooting on the camera.
  • If the camera is not equipped with a movie shooting mode, this function is not available.
